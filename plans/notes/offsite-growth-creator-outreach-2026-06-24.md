## Creator tiering (who to pay, who to gift)

Mega-creators (Anomaly 3.3M, ohnePixel, Sparkles 2.5M, NadeKing 1.4M) = too expensive, low intent-match, skip for now. Target **mid/small skins-focused channels (5k–250k subs)** that do trade-up / "is this profitable" / skin-investing content — their viewers are buyers, their rates are reachable ($50–500/video or free-access-for-honest-review), and a tool mention feels native.

**Two offers, by tier:**
1. **Gift / affiliate (0–50k subs):** free Pro + a unique referral link; you pay nothing up front, they earn per signup. Low risk, scales.
2. **Flat sponsor ($100–500, 50k–250k subs):** a 30–60s integration: "I used TradeUpBot to find this trade-up." Pay per video, not per month.
